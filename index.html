The Heart Behind
Marcella Restaurant

Named after Chef José Sánchez's beloved grandmother, Marcella Restaurant is more than a culinary destination—it's a tribute to family, tradition, and the art of cooking with love.

The violet orchid, Marcella's favorite flower, weaves through every aspect of our restaurant, symbolizing the beauty, elegance, and passion we pour into every dish. Since beginning his journey in 2009, Chef Sánchez has traveled the world, learning from master chefs and bringing international techniques back to his Puerto Rican roots.

At Marcella, we celebrate creative Creole gastronomy, crafting innovative dishes from the freshest local ingredients sourced directly from Puerto Rican farmers and producers. Every plate tells a story, every flavor evokes a memory.
